Meet Our veterinarians

Dr. John Hawley

Welcome, my name is Dr. John Hawley. I graduated from the University of Saskatchewan with a Doctorate in Veterinary Medicine. I specialized in the field of wild and exotic animals gaining experience at both the Calgary and Washington D.C. Zoo. Upon joining the GATINEAU VETERINARY CLINIC Clinic in 1993, I refocused my skills to include orthopedic, soft tissue and internal medicine.

Dr. Marie- Claude Lepage

Hi, I am Dr. Marie- Claude Lepage. I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Animal Physiology and my veterinary degree at Montreal University. I moved to Gatineau in 2005. The wonderful staff at GATINEAU VETERINARY CLINIC have made this an easy transition. My veterinary interests include emergency medicine, ultrasound and soft tissue surgery

Dr Will Rotenberg

Hello, my name is Will Rotenberg. I graduated from Toronto University School of Veterinary Medicine. I practiced in Ontario for a few years in general practice and emergency medicine. There began my strong passion for internal medicine and surgery. I have two dogs, Karma and Ryder – Karma is a Mastiff and Ryder is an Australian Shepherd
